Founding of the Theatre

McPhail's Theatre was founded with a vision to bring the magic of musical theater to the vibrant community of St. Louis. The theater was the brainchild of a group of passionate theater enthusiasts who recognized the city's rich cultural heritage and its love for the arts.

Through their determination and unwavering dedication, the founders successfully launched McPhail's Theatre. Since its inception, the theater has grown and flourished, earning a reputation for its exceptional productions, commitment to artistic excellence, and its role as a cultural hub within the St. Louis community.

Our Mission Statement

We strive for artistic excellence in all aspects of our productions. From casting and direction to design and technical execution, we are dedicated to presenting top-notch performances that rival the best in the industry. We continuously push artistic boundaries, embracing innovation and creativity while honoring the rich traditions of musical theater.

We believe in the power of theater to unite and uplift our community. We actively engage with diverse individuals, organizations, and schools, fostering partnerships that promote cultural appreciation, inclusivity, and collaboration. Through our outreach programs, educational initiatives, and community events, we aim to inspire, educate, and foster a lifelong love for the performing arts.
